## The FAIR Principles

- **F — Findable** · persistent IDs, rich indexed metadata
- **A — Accessible** · open standard protocols (HTTPS)
- **I — Interoperable** · shared vocabularies / ontologies
- **R — Reusable** · licence, provenance, community standards

<!--
Speaker note: A repository gives you F and A almost for free. The I and R — good metadata,
ontology terms, a licence — are the researcher's job. That boundary is the whole workshop.
-->

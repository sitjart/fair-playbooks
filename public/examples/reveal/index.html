The FAIR principles

F
Findable
Persistent IDs · rich, indexed metadata
A
Accessible
Open, standard protocols (HTTPS)
I
Interoperable
Shared vocabularies / ontologies
R
Reusable
Licence · provenance · community standards
A repository gives you F and A almost for free. The I and R — good metadata, ontology terms, a licence — are the researcher's job. That boundary is the whole workshop.

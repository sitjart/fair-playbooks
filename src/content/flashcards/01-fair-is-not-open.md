---
question: "True or false: FAIR means data must be openly available to everyone."
category: general
difficulty: 1
relatedModule: 00-what-is-fair
---

**False.** FAIR is about clarity of access, not openness. Data can be FAIR and access-controlled — what matters is that the access conditions and protocols are clearly described, machine-readable, and consistently applied. A clinical dataset behind a data access committee can still be FAIR.

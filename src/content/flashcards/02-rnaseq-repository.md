---
question: "A researcher has RNA-seq data they want to share. What's the single most impactful action you can recommend?"
category: findable
difficulty: 1
relatedModule: 02-public-repos
---

**Deposit it in ENA, GEO or SRA.** A community repository handles findability (accession), accessibility (download protocols), interoperability (metadata schema) and reusability (community-standard format) in one step. Spend the rest of the conversation on metadata quality, not on bespoke FAIR strategies.

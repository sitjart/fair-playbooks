---
question: "A retiring PI says 'my data is from 2018, it's too late to make it FAIR'. How do you respond?"
category: general
difficulty: 2
relatedModule: 04-late-to-the-party
---

**It's never too late.** The 80/20 of late FAIRification takes a few hours: mint a DOI via Zenodo, write a README describing what the data is and how it was produced, add an explicit license (CC-BY 4.0), and include contact details for follow-up questions. That alone moves a dataset from "lost forever" to "discoverable, reusable, citeable."

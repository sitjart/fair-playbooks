The cultural barrier from the proposal — *"RDM is often perceived as an administrative requirement"* — lands hardest when RDM professionals are talking to leadership. This module is about the language and framings that move that conversation.

**What this module will cover when drafted:**

- The handful of framings that actually land with leadership (citation rates, funder mandates, institutional reputation, reproducibility crisis, risk)
- The framings that backfire (virtue, "doing the right thing", technical compliance, FAIR principles as a checklist)
- How to translate metrics RDM professionals care about into metrics leadership cares about
- A short script for the "30-second pitch in the corridor" scenario
- A longer script for "I have 15 minutes with the head of department"
- What to do when leadership says "yes in principle" but doesn't act
